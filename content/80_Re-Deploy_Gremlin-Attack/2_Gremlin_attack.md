#### Unleash Gremlin


From the Gremlin Dashboard Click Attack

1. Choose a Gremlin 
 - Leave Catagory selected to __State__

2. Chose Attack __Shutdown__

3. Set Delay to __0__

4. Set Reboot to __OFF__

5. Unleash Gremlin
